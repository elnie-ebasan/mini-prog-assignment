More to Discover

Palaypay Falls

Located in Brgy. Abante Pamplona. The cold water has sulfur content but it is safe for swimming. It is just a 24 minute ride from the town and you can enjoy the red rock beauty of the falls.

Canlunggad Falls

This is the biggest and tallest falls in Pamplona. You can find it in Sitio Canlunggad, San Isidro Pamplona. Though it's far from the road but it's totally a must try!

Sakada Farm Restaurant

Another one from Brgy. Abante - a relaxing place to dine with family and friends.

Kan-anan Restaurant

A resto by the road. You can check this popular restaurant at Brgy. Balayong, Pamplona just beside the main road and try their mouth-watering menus

Rabbiton

Animal lover? This is the place for you! It's just 17 minutes away from the town of Pamplona. You'll get to see different kinds of rabbits - pet and feed them all you want without worrying where to eat since they also serve the best foods in town.

Magsusunog River

Another refreshment you must try that the town can offer anyone is the Magsusunog river. Best site for picnics and swimming.

S-Pot View Deck

If you simply need to clear up your mind you can climb up this place and witness the beauty of nature. The best way to find your peace is be alone with nature.

What To Do!

Camping

Want to experience the coldness of the night? Camp at Montefrio! It offers overnight stay for you, your family and/or your friends.

ATV Ride

Enjoy riding and exploring the mountains of Pamplona with this ATV ride. Available for rent at Cafe Alicia for a low price and you can ride anywhere within the designated area.
